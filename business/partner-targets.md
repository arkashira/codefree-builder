## Partner Integration Roadmap for Codefree-Builder

### Target Integrations

| Partner/API             | Free-Tier Limits                  | Integration Effort | Value-Add (User Job)                                      | Affiliate/Revenue-Share |
|-------------------------|-----------------------------------|--------------------|----------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------|
| **Zapier**              | 5 Zaps, 100 Tasks/month           | M                  | Automate workflows between apps without coding.          | Yes                      |
| **Airtable**            | 1,200 records per base            | S                  | Manage and organize data visually, enhancing project management. | Yes                      |
| **Stripe**              | No free tier, but test mode available | M                  | Simplify payment processing for users' applications.     | Yes                      |
| **Twilio**              | 1,000 SMS messages/month          | M                  | Enable communication features (SMS, voice) in apps.     | Yes                      |
| **Slack**               | 10 integrations, 5GB storage      | S                  | Facilitate team collaboration and notifications.         | Yes                      |
| **Google Sheets**       | 15GB storage                      | S                  | Use as a database for app data, easy to manage and visualize. | No                       |
| **Mailgun**             | 5,000 emails/month for 3 months   | M                  | Integrate email capabilities for user applications.      | Yes                      |
| **Notion**              | 1,000 blocks                      | S                  | Provide a collaborative workspace for project documentation. | No                       |

### Rationale

1. **Zapier**: Integrating with Zapier allows users to connect Codefree-Builder with thousands of other apps, automating repetitive tasks and enhancing productivity without the need for coding knowledge. The affiliate program can also generate revenue.

2. **Airtable**: This integration provides users with a powerful database solution that is visually appealing and easy to use, helping them manage their projects and data effectively. The affiliate program adds a revenue-sharing opportunity.

3. **Stripe**: As a leading payment processing platform, integrating Stripe will enable users to monetize their applications easily. Although there is no free tier, the test mode allows for initial integration testing without costs.

4. **Twilio**: By integrating Twilio, users can add communication features to their applications, such as SMS notifications, which are crucial for user engagement. The affiliate program can provide additional revenue.

5. **Slack**: Integrating Slack allows users to receive notifications and collaborate with their teams directly from their applications, enhancing communication and project management. 

6. **Google Sheets**: While it doesn't have an affiliate program, Google Sheets serves as a familiar and accessible database for users, allowing them to manage their data without needing complex setups.

7. **Mailgun**: This integration allows users to add email capabilities to their applications easily, enabling effective communication with their end-users. The affiliate program can help generate revenue.

8. **Notion**: Although it lacks an affiliate program, Notion provides a collaborative environment for users to document their projects, enhancing organization and clarity in their development process. 

### Conclusion
These integrations will not only enhance the functionality of Codefree-Builder but also provide significant value to users by solving key jobs they need to accomplish, while also offering potential revenue-sharing opportunities through affiliate programs.

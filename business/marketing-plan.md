## 3. Channels & CAC Estimates  

| Channel | Tactics (first 90 days) | Expected CPL | Conversion to Paid (≈ % of leads) | CAC* |
|---------|------------------------|--------------|-----------------------------------|------|
| **Paid Community Ads** (e.g., Indie Hackers, Product Hunt, Reddit r/SideProject) | Sponsored posts + retargeting carousel | $0.85 | 8 % | **$10.6** |
| **Content‑Driven SEO + Guest Posts** (e.g., “No‑code MVP in 48h” guide) | Long‑form guide, pillar page, backlinks from SaaS blogs | $0 (organic) | 3 % (from 10 k monthly visitors) | **$7.5** |
| **Partner Referral Program** (e.g., Bubble, Webflow, Zapier) | Co‑branded webinars, revenue‑share on referrals | $0 (partner cost) | 12 % (high intent) | **$4.2** |

\*CAC = (Ad spend + content production cost + partner payout) ÷ number of paying customers acquired in the period.  
Assumptions: 30 % of CAC allocated to content creation ($5 k total), 70 % to paid ads ($35 k total).
